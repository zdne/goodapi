THE TAO OF GOOD API DESIGN

DESCRIBE YOUR DATA

What things you may get and if you get them, what they mean. This is the semantic definition of you application domain.

DESCRIBE WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH THE DATA

What actions your data may afford including any description of action attributes.

IN A WAY BOTH HUMANS AND MACHINES CAN READ

Humans are bad at reading schemas. So are machines at reading a plain text. Yet, you want everbody and every machine to understand (why?).
