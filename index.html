Tao of GOOD API DESIGN

DESCRIBE YOUR DATA

What things you may get and what they mean. This is the semantic definition of you application domain.

DESCRIBE WHAT YOU CAN DO WITH THE DATA

What actions data may afford including any attributes. This is an integral part of application domain semantics.

IN A WAY BOTH HUMANS AND MACHINES CAN READ IT

Humans are bad at reading schemas. Machines are having hard times analyzing speech or plain text. Yet, you want everbody and everymachine to understand.
